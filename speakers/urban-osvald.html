Urban Osvald

0xcert (CCO)

Urban Osvald, CSO at 0xcert. We are developing powerful tools for NFT adoption but are also building an ecosystem around it (with initiatives like The Non-fungible Alliance).

Beyond CryptoKitties: history, data and use cases in the NFT space (talk)

Anyone working / interested in the NFT space. Provides in-depth overview of use cases, data and evolution of NFTs. Key points: CryptoKitties are just the beginning; gaming and collectables are a catalyst for adoption; low user base - data view (in cooperation with nonfungible.com and dappradar.com), UX problems, use cases that connect physical assets and NFTs.
